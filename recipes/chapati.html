Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Water

Steps

  1. Put the flour in a bowl
  2. Mix sugar and olive oil
  3. Add water and knead till it is dough
  4. Leave dough to rest covered for 30 minutes
  5. Roll out the dough into appropriate sized circles
  6. Add a teaspon of oil to frying pan and heat till warm
  7. Fry chapati on both sides until golden brown
